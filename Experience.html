Experience/ Leadership

I would clean the building, perform tasks needed for the business, and learned how to fix a few things on the trucks.

Clubs/Service

I have been apart of United Sound, Latino Advocacy, and Model UN. I was a Volunteer Representative, in United Sound, and taught the violin to high school students with disabilities. I was a Volunteer Representative, in the Latino Advocacy Club, I prepared and set up festivals to help advocate for Latino culture in my school. Lastly, I was a Representative, in Model UN, I prepared debates about Space Exploration in Greece, and made policies concerning the issues at hand.
